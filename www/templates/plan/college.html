What is College

Your plan can be one of these options

Military: serving our country in the military is a great option that allows for training and pays for college after service.

Career Schools: Apprenticeships, and specific job training are paths that you can do right out of high school.

Two Year College: Associates degree, level 1 or 2 certificates are a great option for you to stack those credentials as you work in the field of study and earn your degree.

Four Year University: Achieving your Bachelor’s, Master’s, and Doctorate are paths to great careers.
