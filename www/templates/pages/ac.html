Amarillo College

If Amarillo College is your destination there are some steps you’ll need to take, and they are listed below.

If you need assistance please contact Amarillo College at 806.371.5000 or online at www.actx.edu/contact. They will help you complete the requirement listed below. These requirements can be completed in any order.

Apply for financial aid & scholarships

Scholarships, grants, and loans are available. Applications are free at ww.actx.edu/foundation and fafsa.gov. More financial aid details: www.actx.edu/fin

Submit Official Transcript

Contact your high school counselor and/or the Registrar’s Office at each college you have attended previously to request an official transcript be sent to Amarillo College, Registrar’s Office, P.O. Box 447, Amarillo TX 79178

Provide proof of bacterial meningitis vaccination

New students under the age of 22 are required to show proof of bacterial meningitis vaccinations or complete an exemption form prior to registration.

Arrange Placement Testing

You may be exempt from taking the TSI Assessment. Please speak with an academic advisor or go online at www.actx.edu/testing to check your exemption status. If you are not exempt, you will need to complete the following:

  1. Mandatory Pre-assessment (MAPS) Orientation: New students are required to attend a MAP session in person prior to placement testing. Location: Success Center, Ware Students Commons, 3rd floor, Washington street Campus. www.actx.edu/successcenter 806-345-5542
  2. Placement test: For more information and to schedule a testing time, call 806-371-5445 or visit www.actx.edu/testing. Testing times will also be scheduled after the completion of MAP session.
