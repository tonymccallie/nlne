    Assess patient health problems and needs, develop and implement nursing care plans, and maintain medical records.

  • Carpenter

  • Construction Equipment Operators

    Operate one or several types of power construction equipment.

  • Radiologic Technologist

    Take x rays and CAT scans or administer nonradioactive materials into patient`s blood stream for diagnostic purposes.

  • Surgical Technologist

    Assist in operations, under the supervision of surgeons, registered nurses, or other surgical personnel.

  • Auto Body Technician

    Repair and refinish automotive vehicle bodies and straighten vehicle frames.
